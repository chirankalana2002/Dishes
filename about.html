Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 inch ginger (minced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and cook until soft and slightly golden.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, then sauté for about 1 minute.
  4. Add curry leaves and chopped tomato. Cook until the tomato becomes soft.
  5. Add curry powder, chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the chicken pieces and stir to coat them with the spices.
  7. Cook the chicken for about 5 minutes while stirring.
  8. Pour in the coconut milk and mix well.
  9. Cover and let the curry simmer for 15–20 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked.
  10. Taste and adjust salt if needed.
  11. Serve hot with rice or bread.
