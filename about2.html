Vegetable Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lettuce (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup cucumber (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup carrots (grated)
  • 1/4 cup red onion (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash all the vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop the lettuce, cucumber, and tomatoes into small pieces.
  3. Grate the carrots and slice the red onion.
  4. Add all the vegetables into a large bowl.
  5. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the salad.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Toss everything together until well mixed.
  8. Serve fresh as a healthy side dish.
