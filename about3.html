Mixed Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup chopped beans
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add carrots, beans, and peas, then cook for a few minutes until slightly soft.
  4. If using egg, crack it into the pan and scramble it with the vegetables.
  5. Add the cooked rice and mix well with the vegetables.
  6. Pour soy sauce over the rice and stir well.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Cook for another 2–3 minutes while stirring.
  9. Serve hot.
