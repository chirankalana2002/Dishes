Mushroom Curry

```html

Ingredients

  • 200g mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 inch ginger (minced)
  • 1 green chili (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and cook until soft.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and green chili. Sauté for about 1 minute.
  4. Add curry leaves and chopped tomato. Cook until the tomato softens.
  5. Add curry powder, chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the sliced mushrooms and stir well with the spices.
  7. Cook for about 5 minutes until the mushrooms start to soften.
  8. Pour in the coconut milk and mix well.
  9. Let the curry simmer for about 8–10 minutes.
  10. Taste and adjust salt if needed.
  11. Serve hot with rice or bread.
```
