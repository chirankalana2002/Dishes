Makaroni

Ingredients

  • 2 cups macaroni
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (diced)
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the macaroni in salted water for about 8–10 minutes until soft, then drain the water.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until soft and fragrant.
  4. Add carrots, peas, and bell pepper. Cook for a few minutes until slightly soft.
  5. Add the boiled macaroni to the pan and mix with the vegetables.
  6. Pour in the milk and stir well.
  7. Add grated cheese, salt, and pepper.
  8. Cook for 2–3 minutes while stirring until the cheese melts and everything is well combined.
  9. Serve hot.
