Chicken Cuttless

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (grated)
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Heat a little oil in a pan and sauté onion, garlic, and green chili until soft.
  2. Add shredded chicken, grated carrots, curry powder, pepper, and salt. Cook for 3–4 minutes.
  3. Mix the cooked chicken mixture with mashed potatoes in a bowl.
  4. Shape the mixture into small round or oval cutlets.
  5. Dip each cutlet in beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  6. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  7. Fry the cutlets until golden brown on both sides.
  8. Remove and place on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  9. Serve hot with ketchup or chili sauce.
