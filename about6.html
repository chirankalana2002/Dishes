Egg Curry

Ingredients

  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 inch ginger (minced)
  • 1 green chili (sliced)
  • 1 medium tomato (chopped)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the eggs in water for about 10 minutes. Remove the shells and make small cuts on the eggs so the curry can absorb the flavor.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and cook until they become soft and slightly golden.
  4. Add minced garlic, ginger, and sliced green chili. Stir and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add curry leaves and chopped tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes become soft and start to break down.
  6. Add curry powder, chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook the spices for about 1 minute.
  7. Pour in the coconut milk and stir well to combine the spices with the milk.
  8. Let the curry simmer for about 5 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  9. Add the boiled eggs to the curry and gently mix so the eggs are coated with the sauce.
  10. Cook for another 3–5 minutes to allow the eggs to absorb the flavors.
  11. Taste the curry and adjust salt if needed.
  12. Remove from heat and serve hot with rice or bread.
