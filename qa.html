Questions & Answers

1. What is DishDiary?

DishDiary is a website where you can find and share delicious recipes.

2. How can I add a recipe?

Click the "Add Recipe" page and submit your recipe with ingredients and instructions.

3. Can I search recipes?

Yes, you can use the search bar on the home page to find recipes quickly.

4. What types of recipes are available?

You can find many recipes like chicken curry, prawn curry, mushroom curry, mixed rice and more.

5. Is DishDiary free to use?

Yes, DishDiary is completely free for everyone to use.
